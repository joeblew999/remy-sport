## https://www.fiba.basketball/en/events

### Event Calendar & Discovery
- Monthly calendar view with event counts per month
- Events spanning multiple years (through 2029)
- Regional filtering (World, Africa, Americas, Asia, Europe, Oceania)
- Sport type filtering (Basketball, 3x3)
- Event listings with date ranges and host city/country

### Competition Types Supported
- World Cups and World Cup Qualifiers
- Continental championships (EuroBasket, AmeriCup, AfroBasket)
- Club competitions (EuroLeague, EuroCup, Champions League)
- Youth divisions (U16, U17, U18, U19, U20)
- 3x3 tournaments
- eFIBA (esports)
- Pre-qualifiers and qualifying rounds

### Games & Results
- Daily game schedule with date-based navigation
- Regional and competition-type filtering (National Team, Leagues, 3x3, eFIBA)
- Gender categories (Men, Women, Boys, Girls)
- Live game counter and status indicators (Live, Half-Time, Finished)
- Score displays with quarter/period breakdowns (Q1–Q4, OT)
- Hide Scores toggle to avoid spoilers
- Past events archive

### Rankings System
- World rankings presented by tier (Men, Women, Boys, Girls)
- Regional zone rankings
- Points-based ranking with rank variation (+/- movement)
- Historical rankings data (snapshots from 2010 onward)
- Climbers and Droppers sections (biggest movers)
- Methodology documentation, FAQ, and detailed examples
- 160+ countries ranked

### Fantasy & Fan Engagement
- SMART Fantasy League for World Cup Qualifiers
- FIBA Fan Forum
- "Find a Court" feature
- Follow teams functionality
- Account registration for personalized experience

### Streaming & Media
- Courtside 1891 platform for live and on-demand viewing
- YouTube embedded video highlights
- Photo content and news articles
- Power Rankings editorial content (by competition)

### Social Media Integration
- YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Twitter/X, Threads, LinkedIn, Twitch

- Share functionality

### Player & Team Data
- Player profiles
- Team rosters and information
- Team statistics

### Ticketing & Registration
- Ticket availability and registration for major events (e.g., World Cup 2027)
- Event registration links
